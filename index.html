--- layout: homepage title: CFPB Design Manual category: Overview --- Welcome to the CFPB Design Manual. The Design Manual is an open-source resource built to help employees and contractors produce effective and visually-consistent print and digital products. The Manual includes our design principles, guidelines for user experience, identity standards, and code snippets for common user interface elements. The Manual will continue to evolve as we learn what works best for the CFPB and the people we serve. Help us make improvements We have designed the Manual to be open for the public, which allows you to help us make improvements. Other Resources Many style guides influenced the creation and organization of the Manual, including those from Mozilla, MailChimp, and the BBC. Our primary inspiration is the UK's Government Service Design Manual. We hope ours will serve as a step toward improving user-centered design across all US government websites and services.
